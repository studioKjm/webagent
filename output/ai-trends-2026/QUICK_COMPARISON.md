## Quick Decision Guide

### Choose Dark & Modern (V1) if you answer YES to:
- [ ] Target audience includes developers/engineers
- [ ] Product is technical (AI/ML, dev tools)
- [ ] Brand wants to appear cutting-edge
- [ ] Comfortable with dark interfaces
- [ ] Want to stand out in tech space

### Choose Bold & Vibrant (V2) if you answer YES to:
- [ ] Target audience is general consumers
- [ ] Need maximum visual impact
- [ ] Brand is energetic and disruptive
- [ ] Social media virality is a goal
- [ ] Youth market is primary target

### Choose Minimal & Clean (V3) if you answer YES to:
- [ ] Target audience is executives/decision-makers
- [ ] Product is enterprise/B2B
- [ ] Trust and credibility are paramount
- [ ] Professional presentation required
- [ ] Industry is conservative (finance, legal, healthcare)
